Legislative Assembly

1 A BILL for an Act to amend and reenact subsection 1 of section 15.1-21-01 and subsection 1 of
2 section 15.1-21-02 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to requiring the presidential
3 physical fitness test in physical education courses in elementary, middle, and high schools; and
4 to provide an effective date.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY OF NORTH DAKOTA:
6 SECTION 1. AMENDMENT. Subsection 1 of section 15.1-21-01 of the North Dakota
7 Century Code is amended and reenacted as follows:
8 1. Provide to students instruction in:
9 a. English language arts, including reading, composition, creative writing, English
10 grammar, and spelling.
11 b. Mathematics.
12 c. Social studies, including:
13 (1) The United States Constitution;
14 (2) United States history;
15 (3) Geography;
16 (4) Government; and
17 (5) North Dakota studies, with an emphasis on geography, history, the federally
18 recognized Indian tribes in the state, and agriculture of this state, in the
19 fourth and eighth grades.
20 d. Science, including agriculture.
21 e. Physical education, including requiring participation in the presidential physical
22 fitness test.
23 f. Health, including physiology, hygiene, disease control, and the nature and effects
24 of alcohol, tobacco, and narcotics.
